Page Design

  • Home Page
    • Purpose: To be a homepage the art portfolio of Hannah Grubb
    • Audience: People wanting to view the art portfolio of Hannah Grubb
    • Content: A menu linking to the other pages as well as a description of the website
    • Interactivity: None
  • About Page
    • Purpose: To provide information about the artist
    • Audience: People wanting to view the art portfolio of Hannah Grubb
    • Content: A description of the artist and their art style
    • Interactivity: None
  • Artwork Page
    • Purpose: To provide a gallery of the artist's work
    • Audience: People wanting to view the art portfolio of Hannah Grubb
    • Content: A gallery of the artist's work in the form of a slideshow
    • Interactivity: None
  • Contact Page
    • Purpose: To provide a way for the artist to be contacted
    • Audience: People wanting to view the art portfolio of Hannah Grubb
    • Content: A form for the artist to be contacted
    • Interactivity: None
  • Commission Page
    • Purpose: To provide a way for the artist to be commissioned
    • Audience: People wanting to commission the artist
    • Content: A form for the artist to be commissioned
    • Interactivity: Accept a prompt from the user and a contact method. The contact method will need to be required and validated, The prompt will also be required.
  • Print Order Page
    • Purpose: To provide a way for the artist to sell prints of certain works
    • Audience: People wanting to view the art portfolio of Hannah Grubb
    • Content: A form for the artist to sell prints
    • Interactivity: Accept an order from the user and a contact method. The contact method will need to be required and validated.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Have artwork displayed in a slideshow and have zoom and gallery options on the artwork page
  • Have a form that will accept a prompt from the user and a contact method. Javascript will be used to validate the inputs. This will be on the commission page
  • Form to accept print orders including work and numbers wanted. Will also generate a estimated quote. This will be on the Print order page.
