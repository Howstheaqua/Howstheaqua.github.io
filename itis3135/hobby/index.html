Parts

This section will identify all the different parts that go into constructing a mechanical keyboard. The most important part that makes these keyboards what they are are the key switches. These are the parts that go under the keys and activate to signal that you have pressed that key. These are mechanical switches and thats what gives the keyboard its name. The next most important part is the PCB(Printed Ciruct Board), which is where the swtiches are attachted to
